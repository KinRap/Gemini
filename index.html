Open Your Box Like a Pro!

Simple Steps:

  1. Hold the box firmly with both hands.
  2. Locate the top flaps where they meet at the top.
  3. Gently lift the flaps on both sides of the box.
  4. Carefully unfold the flaps completely to reveal the contents.

Pro Tips for Smooth Opening:

  • Maintain a steady grip to prevent tipping.
  • Apply gentle, even pressure while lifting the flaps.
  • Lift the flaps straight up to avoid tearing.

What to Do with the Box After Opening:

  • Dispose of packaging responsibly (check local recycling guidelines).
  • Reuse the box for storage or shipping (consider creative reuse ideas!).
